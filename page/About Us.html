Welcome to Khmer Traditional Food

Khmer food is more than just cooking — it's a story of culture, heritage, and family. From the famous Fish Amok to refreshing Samlor Sngor Nga Trey, each dish carries the identity of Cambodia through unique spices, herbs, and techniques.

Our Mission

Preserve Tradition

Keeping Khmer culinary heritage alive by sharing authentic recipes.

Inspire Cooking

Encouraging everyone to cook and enjoy Cambodian dishes at home.

Share Culture

Promoting Khmer food worldwide as a unique cultural treasure.

Our Story

Every Khmer dish carries a piece of history — from the royal kitchens of Angkor to humble family gatherings in villages across Cambodia. Food has always been more than nourishment; it is a way of expressing love, celebrating festivals, and preserving traditions that have been passed down for centuries. Each recipe reflects a harmony of local herbs, spices, and techniques that tell the story of our ancestors. Through this blog, we aim to share not only the flavors of Cambodia, but also the cultural identity, wisdom, and sense of community that live within every dish.

Join Our Journey

Be part of our mission to preserve Khmer food traditions and ensure they continue to thrive in the modern world. By exploring, cooking, and sharing your favorite dishes, you help keep the stories of Cambodia alive on every plate. Whether you are a passionate home cook, a curious traveler, or simply someone who loves the taste of authentic flavors, your contribution adds to a growing community that celebrates cultural heritage. Together, we can pass on the joy of Khmer cuisine to the next generation and inspire the world to experience its richness.
