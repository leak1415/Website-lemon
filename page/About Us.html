Welcome to Khmer Traditional Food

Khmer food is more than just cooking — it’s a story of culture, heritage, and family. From the famous Fish Amok to refreshing Samlor Sngor Nga Trey, each dish carries the identity of Cambodia through unique spices, herbs, and techniques.

This blog was created to share authentic recipes, inspire home cooking, and preserve the traditions of Khmer cuisine for future generations. Whether you are a local food lover or an international visitor curious about Cambodian flavors, we welcome you here.

Explore our recipes, learn about the cultural stories behind the dishes, and connect with the vibrant spirit of Cambodia through food.

Our Mission

Preserve Tradition

Keeping Khmer culinary heritage alive by sharing authentic recipes.

Inspire Cooking

Encouraging everyone to cook and enjoy Cambodian dishes at home.

Share Culture

Promoting Khmer food worldwide as a unique cultural treasure.

Our Story

Every Khmer dish carries a piece of history — from the royal kitchens of Angkor to family gatherings in villages. Through this blog, we hope to pass on these stories and flavors to the next generation and the world.

Join Our Journey

Be part of our mission to preserve Khmer food traditions. Explore, cook, and share your favorite dishes with us — together, we’ll keep Cambodia’s culinary spirit alive.
