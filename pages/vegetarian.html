Vegetarian

អាម៉ុកបួស (Steamed Vegetarian Amok)

A creamy, fragrant curry steamed with tofu and vegetables, a healthy vegetarian twist on the classic Khmer dish.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Coconut milk
  • Vegetables (carrot, green beans, pumpkin)
  • Kroeung (Khmer curry paste)
  • Banana leaves for steaming

Steps

  1. Mix coconut milk with kroeung and vegetables.
  2. Add cubed tofu to the mixture.
  3. Pour into banana leaf cups and steam until cooked.
  4. Serve warm with rice.

Benefits

High in protein from tofu, rich in vitamins from vegetables, and low in fat.

Serving & Tips

  • Serve with steamed rice for a complete meal.
  • Adjust spice level to taste.

ការីខ្មែរបួស (Vegetarian Khmer Curry)

A mild Khmer-style curry with seasonal vegetables and light coconut milk, perfect for vegetarians.

Ingredients

  • Mixed vegetables (carrot, potato, pumpkin, beans)
  • Coconut milk
  • Kroeung (vegetarian curry paste)
  • Tofu (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook vegetables in coconut milk and kroeung until tender.
  2. Add tofu if desired and simmer for a few more minutes.
  3. Serve with steamed rice.

Benefits

Rich in fiber, vitamins, and plant-based protein.

Serving & Tips

  • Pair with rice or bread.
  • Adjust seasoning to your taste.

បាញ់សុង

A light vegetarian rice noodle soup with fresh herbs and tofu, refreshing and healthy.

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Vegetable broth
  • Tofu
  • Fresh herbs (cilantro, green onion, basil)
  • Bean sprouts

Steps

  1. Prepare vegetable broth with seasoning.
  2. Cook rice noodles separately.
  3. Combine noodles, tofu, and broth.
  4. Garnish with fresh herbs and bean sprouts.

Benefits

Low-calorie, high in nutrients, and easy to digest.

Serving & Tips

  • Serve hot with lime and chili sauce.
  • Use firm tofu for better texture.

គុយទាវ

Simple and nutritious rice or noodle dish with tofu and assorted vegetables for vegetarians.

Ingredients

  • Steamed rice or rice noodles
  • Tofu, cubed
  • Seasonal vegetables (carrots, beans, cabbage)
  • Soy sauce and spices

Steps

  1. Cook rice or noodles.
  2. Sauté tofu and vegetables with light seasoning.
  3. Serve tofu and vegetables over rice or noodles.

Benefits

Balanced vegetarian meal with protein, fiber, and essential nutrients.

Serving & Tips

  • Add fresh herbs or chili to enhance flavor.
  • Best eaten fresh and warm.

ឆាបន្លែ (Vegetarian Mushroom Salad)

A fresh and tangy mushroom salad, perfect as a light vegetarian dish or appetizer.

Ingredients

  • Sliced mushrooms
  • Fresh herbs (mint, cilantro)
  • Chili and lime dressing
  • Shallots and roasted rice powder

Steps

  1. Mix mushrooms with herbs and shallots.
  2. Add roasted rice powder and chili-lime dressing.
  3. Toss well and serve immediately.

Benefits

Low in calories, high in antioxidants, and rich in flavor.

Serving & Tips

  • Serve chilled as a starter.
  • Adjust spice and lime to taste.

បាយឆា

Khmer-style fried rice cooked with garlic, soy sauce, and fresh vegetables. A quick and flavorful dish loved by everyone.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 2 eggs
  • Garlic, chopped
  • Mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, corn, etc.)
  • Soy sauce & fish sauce
  • Spring onion for garnish
  • Optional: Chicken, pork, or shrimp

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan, fry garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add meat (if using) and cook until done.
  3. Push aside, scramble eggs, then mix with rice.
  4. Add vegetables, season with soy sauce & fish sauce.
  5. Stir-fry until well combined, garnish with spring onion.

Benefits

A quick, filling meal that provides carbohydrates, protein, and vitamins from mixed vegetables.

Serving & Tips

  • Serve hot with cucumber slices and chili sauce.
  • Best made with day-old rice for better texture.
  • Can be customized with your favorite protein.
